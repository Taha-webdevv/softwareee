Frequently Asked Questions

How do I download my purchased software?

After completing your purchase, you'll receive an email with download instructions. You can also access your downloads from your account page under "My Purchases". All software downloads include detailed installation guides.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept all major credit cards (Visa, MasterCard, American Express), PayPal, and Apple Pay. All transactions are secure and encrypted to protect your information.

What is your refund policy?

We offer a 30-day money-back guarantee for all our products. If you're not satisfied with your purchase, please contact our support team to process your refund. Please note that refunds for e-books may be subject to additional terms.

Do you offer technical support for your software?

Yes, we provide technical support for all our software products. Support includes installation assistance, troubleshooting, and basic usage guidance. Premium support plans with extended services are also available for purchase.

Can I use your products on multiple devices?

Our standard license allows you to install the software on up to 2 devices for a single user. For team or enterprise licenses that cover multiple users, please see our licensing options or contact our sales team.
